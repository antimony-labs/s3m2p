Welcome to my digital playground. This is where I experiment with infinite resolution simulations, hardware integrations, and autonomous agents.

Mission

To explore the intersection of biological algorithms and digital infrastructure. This site is a living organism, running entirely in your browser via WebAssembly and Rust.

Architecture

  • Engine: Custom ECS (Entity Component System) written in Rust.
  • Rendering: High-performance Canvas 2D & WebGPU (experimental).
  • Simulation: Real-time Boid Flocking, Fungal Network growth, and Fluid Dynamics.

Modules

  • Helios: Solar system simulation and plasma dynamics.
  • Sensors: Hardware projects and IoT data streams.
  • Learn: Neural networks and ML experiments.
  • Automation: Tools for repetitive task elimination.
